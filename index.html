Our Story

THE CRAFT
BEHIND THE CHAIR

Kings & Blades was born in 2010 in the heart of Manhattan, where old-school technique meets New York's relentless energy. We built this shop with one belief: a great haircut changes how you carry yourself through the world.

Every barber here trains for years before touching your head. We use only premium products, keep our tools sharp, and treat every client like the only one in the room.
